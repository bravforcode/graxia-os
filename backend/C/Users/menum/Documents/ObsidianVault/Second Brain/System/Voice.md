# Voice

Direct and outcome-focused. Lead with the client's problem, not my credentials. Short sentences. Reference their specific situation before any ask. Mention real things I've built — Testlyn, Plexta, or a specific past project — not generic claims about being "passionate" or "experienced." One clear call to action at the end. Nothing more.
