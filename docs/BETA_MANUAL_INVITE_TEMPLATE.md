# Beta Invite — Manual Invite Template

> Phase 20 — Limited Beta Launch Packet
> Use this template when manually inviting a beta tester.
> **Do NOT send automatically.** Each invite is manually crafted and sent by the operator.

---

**Subject:** Invitation to try Graxia OS — Phase 20 Limited Beta

**To:** {tester_email}

Hi {tester_name},

I'm running a limited beta pilot for Graxia OS, and I'd like to invite you to try it.

**What it is:**
An AI assistant that helps with opportunity scouting, outreach drafting, and workflow automation for revenue operations.

**What to expect:**
- This is **alpha-quality software** — you may encounter bugs or rough edges.
- **No SLA** — I'm running this as a pilot, not a production service.
- **No live payments** — all financial features are in sandbox/test mode.
- **All AI output is draft-only** — nothing is sent or published automatically.
- The system may be reset between sessions without notice.

**What I need from you:**
- Use the system for your revenue workflow tasks.
- Report bugs, confusion, and what you find valuable.
- Give feedback on AI recommendations (accurate? useful? off-base?).
- A quick 15-minute debrief call after your first session.

**What's in it for you:**
- Early access to an AI revenue assistant.
- Influence the product direction with your feedback.
- Free during the pilot period.

**Next steps:**
1. Reply to this email if you're interested.
2. I'll send you a quick onboarding checklist and schedule a 30-minute guided first session.
3. After onboarding, you can use the system on your own schedule.

**Questions?** Just reply to this email.

Best,
{operator_name}

## Checklist — Before Sending

- [ ] Tester identified from trusted network (no self-serve signup)
- [ ] Tester fits ideal beta profile (revenue operations / sales / business development)
- [ ] Tester understands alpha-quality expectations
- [ ] Tester agrees to provide feedback
- [ ] Invite limit: **max 3 active testers total**

## After Acceptance

- [ ] Tester has acknowledged the terms
- [ ] Onboarding session scheduled
- [ ] Tester added to beta allowlist (via `BetaRegistry.add_tester()`)
- [ ] Tester activated (`BetaRegistry.activate_tester()`)
- [ ] Tester's initial limits configured in registry
