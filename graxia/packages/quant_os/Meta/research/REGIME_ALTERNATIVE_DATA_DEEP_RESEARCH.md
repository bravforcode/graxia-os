## KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR B2 PAPER TRADING IMPLEMENTATION

### Regime Detection Architecture (from research)
1. **HMM-based regime detection** is the gold standard — papers #1-10 show 2-3 state models (low vol trending, high vol trending, mean-reverting) work best for gold
2. **ADX > 25** is the consensus threshold for regime classification (papers #41-49) — below 25 = ranging/mean-reversion regime
3. **Bollinger Band Width** expansion/contraction confirms regime transitions (papers #50-57) — squeeze → expansion = regime shift
4. **VIX quantile-based regimes** complement technical regime detection (papers #68-76) — VIX > 25 = risk-off regime

### Alternative Data Pipeline (from research)
5. **Sentiment analysis from news** significantly improves gold price forecasting (papers #90-97) — CNN-LSTM and NLP models lead
6. **Gold ETF flow analysis** provides institutional positioning signals (papers #115-121) — inflows correlate with bullish regime
7. **COT report** is still valuable for contrarian signals (papers #122-129) — net spec positioning extremes signal reversals

### Macro Regime Signals (from research)
8. **Real interest rates** are the #1 fundamental driver of gold (papers #136-142) — negative real rates = bullish gold regime
9. **DXY inverse correlation** is regime-dependent (papers #143-150) — correlation weakens during crisis (safe-haven overrides dollar channel)
10. **Central bank buying** is a structural shift — post-2022 regime change (papers #130-135) — de-dollarization trend supports gold floor
11. **Geopolitical risk** creates short-term spikes but gold's safe-haven property is validated across 200+ years (papers #160-170)

### Trading Edge Hierarchy
```
Layer 1: Regime Detection (HMM + ADX + BBW + VIX)
Layer 2: Directional Bias (Real Rates + DXY + Sentiment)
Layer 3: Timing (COT Extremes + ETF Flows + Order Flow)
Layer 4: Risk Management (Volatility Regime + Geopolitical Overlay)
```
