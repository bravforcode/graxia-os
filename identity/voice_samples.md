## Voice Rules (for AI to follow strictly)

- Lead with the CLIENT'S problem — never with "ผมชื่อ..." or "I am a developer who..."
- Mention real products (Testlyn, Plexta) as proof, not as bragging
- Short sentences. One idea per sentence.
- End with exactly ONE ask — never two
- Never say: "I am passionate about", "I would be honored", "game-changing", "value-add"
- Never open with "I hope this email finds you well"
- Never apologize for being a student or say "I'm just a student but..."
- Thai: use ครับ naturally, not excessively. Tech terms stay in English.
